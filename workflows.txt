Page Load
-state is new (out of new, started, stopped)
-init new sequence from length
-init interval boxes from length

Start Button Pressed
-state is started
-disable length selector
-disable speed selector
-wait 500 ms
-loop through sequence from beginning taking into account speed
-for each step turn interval box green

Stop Button Pressed
-state is stopped
-stop sequence

Interval Button Pressed
-if a button is coloured green, change box to that interval
